Explorers are web applications that allow you to quickly obtain information from the blockchain through a friendly user interface. An explorer can help you check the balance of an account, search for a transaction and its receipts, check the history of interactions in an account or contract, and view block creations in real time.

## NearBlocks

Created by the community, [NearBlocks](https://nearblocks.io/) enables you to check accounts and transactions with an interface similar to Etherscan.
